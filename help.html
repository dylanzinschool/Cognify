FAQ's

  • Q: How do I add a new goal?
    A: Use the input field on the Dashboard to type your goal, then click the "Save" button.
  • Q: Can I edit or delete a goal?
    A: You can delete the goal by pressing "Delete" next to your goal, however, you cannot edit them yet!
  • Q: Why is my goal not saving/deleting?
    A: Sometimes refreshing the page should help as your goals are sent off to a database!
  • Q: Do I need an account to use Cognify?
    A: No, Cognify doesn't require an account. Your data is stored in a database.
  • Q: Is my data safe?
    A: Yes! Your data is securely stored with Firebase.
